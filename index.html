About Me

Welcome to Andy Loves Tacos, your go-to source for professional surf photography in Nosara, Costa Rica. I’m Andy, a local surf photographer who has been documenting Nosara’s surf culture since 2015. Most days you'll find me on Playa Guiones capturing surfers of all levels, from first-time wave riders to experienced surfers chasing their best sessions. If you're looking for authentic surf photography in Nosara, you're in the right place.

Why Get Surf Photos?

Nosara is one of Costa Rica’s most photogenic surf destinations, with consistent waves, tropical weather, and beautiful beaches that create incredible conditions for photography. Many surfers invest heavily in their trips, lessons, boards, and travel. Professional photos help preserve those memories long after the trip ends. Capture epic waves, track your progress, analyze your technique, create social media content, celebrate with friends, or preserve family memories in the water.

Photography Services

Choose from Private Surf Photography Sessions for dedicated one-on-one coverage, Group Sessions for surf trips and friends, or Vacation Photography Packages that document your entire stay. Private sessions include focused shooting, high-resolution images, secure online delivery, and a fast turnaround to maximize your chances of getting incredible shots. I also offer photography for families, couples, engagements, maternity, yoga, lifestyle content, events, birthday parties, celebrations, and personal branding.

Daily Galleries & Fast Delivery

One of the things clients love most is same-day photo delivery. Nobody wants to wait weeks to relive an amazing surf session. I regularly photograph surfers in Nosara and upload daily galleries. If you saw me with a camera during your surf session, your wave may already be online. Simply find your photo, send me the image code, purchase the image, and receive a high-resolution, watermark-free download. Great memories, delivered fast.

 

Waves disappear in seconds, but a great photograph lasts forever. Let’s capture your Nosara experience and create images you'll cherish for years to come.
