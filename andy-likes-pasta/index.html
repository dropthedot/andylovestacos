About

Hi there, I’m Andy and I love Tacos! I moved to Nosara in 2015, most days you’ll find me in the water or on the beach with a camera in my hands. Surf Photography isn’t a side thing for me, it’s part of my daily life. When I’m not shooting, I’m usually surfing or hanging with my family. I’ve got three kids who I love and who keep me busy livin' the Dad life. From Playa Grande to Camaronal, I’m always moving along the coast, stoked when I find clean conditions and good people. Send me a message if you have any questions about the beaches along the coast here. If I end up shooting you, you can expect images that feel natural and honest. Stoked to share the lineup with you!

Cheers!
Andy
