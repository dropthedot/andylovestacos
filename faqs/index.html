FAQ'S

What if the surf conditions are no good on our scheduled shoot date?

If we have poor conditions I am happy to reschedule at no additional cost.

When do I get my photos?

I will send you a download link within 4 hours of finishing the shoot.

Will you travel to a different beach with me or my group?

Yes I am happy to surf trip with you, I will drive separately and meet you at your desired beach. I am familiar and happy to shoot any beach from Camaronal to Playa Grande. This does have an additional cost depending on which beach and the time of the year.
